Connect to Spotify

You'll need a free Spotify Developer app to get your API credentials. Takes about 2 minutes.

1
Go to developer.spotify.com/dashboard and create a new app.
2
In the app settings, add https://musicplayer.test:8888/callback as a Redirect URI and save.
3
Copy your Client ID and Client Secret from the app dashboard.
Stored locally on your machine. Never shared.
Redirect URI to add in Spotify:
https://musicplayer.test:8888/callback
