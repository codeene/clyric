Setup takes about 2 minutes. You'll need a free Spotify Developer account.

Create a Spotify App

Now Playing needs a Spotify Developer app to read your currently playing track. It's free and takes 2 minutes.

1
Go to developer.spotify.com/dashboard and click Create app.
2
Give it any name and description. Under APIs used, check Web API.
3
In Redirect URIs, paste exactly:

https://musicplayer.test:8888/callback
4
Click Add then scroll down and hit Save.

Enter your credentials

From your Spotify app dashboard, copy your Client ID and Client Secret.

Stored locally on your machine. Never shared.
